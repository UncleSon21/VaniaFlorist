Care Instructions

To ensure your flowers stay fresh and beautiful for as long as possible:

  1. Remove flowers from packaging immediately
  2. Cut 2-3cm off stems at a 45-degree angle
  3. Remove any leaves below the water line
  4. Place in clean vase with fresh, cool water
  5. Add flower food if provided
  6. Change water every 2-3 days
  7. Keep away from direct sunlight and heating
